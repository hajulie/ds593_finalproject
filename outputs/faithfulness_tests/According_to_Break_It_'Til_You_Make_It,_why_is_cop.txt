Question: According to Break It 'Til You Make It, why is copyright law not a complete solution for regulating AI development?

Answer:

According to Break It 'Til You Make It, why is copyright law not a complete solution for regulating AI development?

The answer is that copyright law is not a complete solution for regulating AI development because it does not address the complex legal issues that arise when generative AI systems train on copyrighted works. Copyright law is notoriously complicated, and generative-AI systems manage to touch on a great many corners of it. These systems raise issues of author-ship, similarity, direct and indirect liability, and fair use, among many other legal issues. Therefore, copyright law is not a complete solution for regulating AI development.
